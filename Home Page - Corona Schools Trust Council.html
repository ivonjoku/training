A Legacy of World-Class Education

We are in tune with the future of learning, and we continue to lead the pack in world-class education in Nigeria.

Our mission: To provide world-class education to children. We inculcate high moral and ethical values in our students as we prepare them for lifelong learning, service and fulllment.

Our vision: To be Nigerias leading educational institution focused on and dedicated to producing well-rounded and proudly Nigerian young men and women equipped for continuous learning, personal mastery and leadership.

About us

Why Corona Schools?

Nursery Schools' Curriculum

We operate the Montessori method which support pupils' learning process in all curriculum areas.

Primary Schools' Curriculum

A blend of Nigerian and other international curricula and is carefully aligned with the International Primary Curriculum (IPC).

Secondary Schools' Curriculum

A blend of Nigerian and British curricula to equip students for entry to top advanced level undergraduate programmes across the world.

World Class School

Corona Secondary School Agbara is the only Secondary School in Africa to be Accredited by NEASC with highest standards that are globally recognised.

ACCA

Only Secondary school students in Nigeria to pass the foundation level of the Association of Chartered Certified Accountant (ACCA).

British Council Award

Corona schools won Community Initiative Award 2019 for its student-led water borehole initiative.

Co-curricular Activities

Music, Taekwondo, Athletics, Ballet, Golf, Cheerleading, Dance, Basketball, Jolly Phonics, Hausa, Igbo, Yoruba, Drama, French, Reading, Football, Chess, Steam, Music, Go Techy (Animation), Athletics, ICT, STEAM, Basketball, Public Speaking/Literary & Debating, Reading, Cooking Club, Model United Nations, Jolly Phonics/Elocution, Young Writers Workshop, Brownies, and German, among others. Corona School Gbagada is a foremost Rugby School in Lagos State. 4th time winner of the trophy since the inception of Tag-Rugby in the state.

The focus of the Corona Schools Trust Council is the development of world-class schools. World-class in all facets of our operations curriculum, teaching methodologies, staff quality, libraries, technology, infrastructure, and so on.
